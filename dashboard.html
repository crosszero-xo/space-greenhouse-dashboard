About This Project

The Smart Space Greenhouse Monitoring System is an IoT based solution that enables autonomous monitoring and control of plant growth conditions in extraterrestrial environments such as space stations, lunar bases, or Mars habitats.

The system continuously monitors environmental parameters including temperature, humidity, carbon dioxide concentration, soil moisture and light intensity. Data is transmitted to a monitoring dashboard allowing astronauts or researchers to supervise plant health and optimize oxygen production through photosynthesis.
